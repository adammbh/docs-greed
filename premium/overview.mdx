## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is a server snapshot?">
    Snapshots are automatic backups of your server's configuration, roles, and channels. If your server is raided or nuked, you can restore from a recent snapshot.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I manage premium features?">
    Use the Greed dashboard or premium commands to enable, configure, and manage all premium features.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or transfer my premium?">
    Yes! You can upgrade your plan or transfer premium to another server at any time from the dashboard.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What support do I get?">
    Premium users receive priority support and early access to new features. [Join our Discord server](https://discord.gg/greedbot) for help and updates.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
